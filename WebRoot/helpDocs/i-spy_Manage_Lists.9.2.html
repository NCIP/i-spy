Managing Lists Overview
The I-SPY Manage Lists function centralizes all activities pertaining to the creation and management of user-defined, as well as study-defined, PatientDID lists and Gene lists. With these lists, you can further refine queries or facilitate analysis.
Note:
On the Manage Lists page, you can minimize the number of lists displayed by clicking the PatientDID Lists or Gene Lists headings.
