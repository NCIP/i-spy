p53 Search Results
The p53 search results list records that satisfy the search criteria you specified (Performing a p53 Query).
To open and view search results, follow these steps:
1.
On the View Results tab, under Report Results, click the hypertext link showing the name of your query. This opens the I-Spy Post Analysis Portal, showing the report details.
The report includes the following fields: PatientDIDs, Mutation Status, Mutation Type, Exon or Intron Location, Base Change, Codon & Amino Acid Change, and Protein Structure Domain.
