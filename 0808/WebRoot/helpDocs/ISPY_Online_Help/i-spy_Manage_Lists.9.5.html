Removing Data Items to Create a New List
You may delete items from an existing list, then view the new list or save the list. To delete data items, follow these steps:
1.
At the top of the Manage List page, click on the type of lists you would like to view (PatientDID List or Gene List).
2.
Find the list you want to change, and click on the box next to the list name.
3.
Click the details icon to display all the items in the selected list.
4.
Click the delete link beside the item you want to delete.The item is removed from the list.
Once you remove the items, you can view the new list or export the list to your computer. See Exporting a List.
