Looking Up a Patient or Sample Identifier
When you search for a patient identifier, Biospecimen Research Database displays the patient along with all the samples associated with the patient. If you search for a sample identifier, Biospecimen Research Database displays the patient associated with the sample identifier. To perform an ID lookup, follow these steps:
1.
From the ID Lookup page, enter a valid patient identifier, such as 1001, or enter a valid sample identifier, such as 209512.
To enter multiple identifiers, separate the identifiers with commas. For example, enter 1001,1002.
2.
Click the Search button.
The patients associated with the identifier(s) appear below the Search button.
