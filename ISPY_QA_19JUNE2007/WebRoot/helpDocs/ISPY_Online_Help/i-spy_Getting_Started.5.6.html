Getting Help
Information about how to use Biospecimen Research Database is easily accessed from Biospecimen Research Database’s menu in the top left of the Biospecimen Research Database workspace.
The following table describes each item on the Biospecimen Research Database toolbar.
Help
How to Access
Complete online help
To access the complete version of online Biospecimen Research Database help, click the help linklocated in the Biospecimen Research Database menu.
For complete page-level help, click on any Biospecimen Research Database page.
For brief field help, click .
Application support
To obtain support for Biospecimen Research Database, click the support link located under the Biospecimen Research Database menu.
User’s Guide
To access a pdf version of the Biospecimen Research Database User’s Guide, click the user guide link located under the Biospecimen Research Database menu.
For application support, see Application Support.
