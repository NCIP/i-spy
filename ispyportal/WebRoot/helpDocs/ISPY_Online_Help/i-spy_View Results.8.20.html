GenePattern Analysis
The GenePattern Job Result page describes how to process the I-SPY data in GenePattern.
Once the job is completed, click the link to open GenePattern job. The Welcome to GenePattern page appears with general instructions on how to use the application. Refer to Recent Jobs on the right panel for the tasks that you can perform with the I-SPY data.
For complete documentation on how to use GenePattern, click Help on the blue bar at the top of the window.
