Displaying/Hiding the Patient Sample Information
Once you perform an ID Lookup, you can display sample information for either an individual patient or multiple patients. Follow these steps:
1.
To display all the samples collected for an individual patient click next to the patient’s row.
The table highlights the lookup criteria.
The following table describes the sample information associated with the patient.
Item
Special Instructions
ISPY ID
The identifier for the patient.
LabTrak ID
The identifier for the sample collected.
