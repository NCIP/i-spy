Deleting an Entire List
To delete one or more lists from a list type, follow these steps:
1.
At the top of the Manage List page, click on the type of lists you would like to view (PatientDID List or Gene List).
2.
Find the list you want to delete, and click the box next to the list name. Click more than one box to select multiple lists for deletion.
3.
To delete the selected lists, click an x delete icon.
4.
The lists are removed.
