About I-SPY
The NCI Center for Bioinformatics (NCICB), in collaboration with physicians, researchers, and cooperative groups, has designed I-SPY. Clinical trials are critical to identifying markers and mechanisms of resistance in therapy, and I-SPY is a multi-center clinical trial for women undergoing neoadjuvant chemotherapy from breast cancer. I-SPY is a web-based system which supports correlative data analysis and centralized reporting of results to catalyze the transition from uniform to tailored care.
I-SPY facilitates collaboration, provides an infrastructure for data management, analysis and communication, and develops a commitment to sharing information and developing data standards.
