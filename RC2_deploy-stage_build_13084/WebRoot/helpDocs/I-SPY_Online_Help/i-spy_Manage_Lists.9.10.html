Exporting a List
To export a list, follow these steps:
1.
At the top of the Manage List page, click PatientDID Lists or Gene Lists.
2.
Find a list to be exported, and click the details icon to display all of the items in the list.
Note:
To export a list from the side bar, double-click the list name. Open the file in Notepad or save the patient identifiers to a spreadsheet file.
3.
Scroll to the bottom of the list of data items, and click the export list link
4.
Open the list in Notepad or save the list to spreadsheet file on your computer.
